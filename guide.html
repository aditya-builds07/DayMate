User Guide

Everything you need to know about Littlebird Calendar. From basics to power-user tips.

Features Overview

Monthly Calendar View

A beautifully crafted monthly grid with smooth slide transitions when navigating between months. Each cell shows event tags with category colors.

Today Highlight

The current day is always visible with a pulsing golden glow ring. Press T on your keyboard to jump to today instantly.

Event Management

Create, edit, and delete events with an animated modal. Set title, date, time, category, and notes for each event.

Live Stats

The stats bar shows events today, this week, this month, and the next upcoming event. Numbers animate with a counter roll effect.

Search & Filter

Search events by title or notes in real-time. Filter by category using the animated pill buttons above the calendar.

Upcoming Sidebar

A slide-in sidebar panel shows your next upcoming events in chronological order. Click any card to edit the event.

Auto-Save

All events are persisted in your browser's localStorage. No login, no server, no data ever leaves your machine.

4 Categories

Color-coded event categories: Meeting (gold), Personal (green), Reminder (blue), and Deadline (orange). Each with its own glow.

Getting Started

1

Open the Calendar

Click "Open Calendar" on the landing page or navigate directly to the app page. The calendar loads with today's month and pre-populated sample events.

2

Navigate Months

Use the left and right arrow buttons next to the month title, or press the Left/Right arrow keys on your keyboard. The calendar slides smoothly in the direction of travel.

3

Create an Event

Click any date cell on the calendar, or press the "New Event" button in the top bar, or press N on your keyboard. Fill in the title, pick a date, time, and category, then click Save.

4

Edit or Delete Events

Click a date cell that has events to open the first event for editing. Modify any field and click Save, or click Delete to remove the event. A toast notification will confirm the action.

5

View Upcoming Events

Click the grid icon in the top-right corner or press S to toggle the sidebar. It shows your next 8 upcoming events in order, with animated card entrances.

6

Search & Filter

Type in the search bar to filter events by title or notes. Use the category pills (Meeting, Personal, Reminder, Deadline) to show only specific event types.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Key Action Description
← Previous Month Navigate to the previous month with a slide animation
→ Next Month Navigate to the next month with a slide animation
T Go to Today Jump back to the current month and highlight today
N New Event Open the event creation modal for today's date
S Toggle Sidebar Open or close the upcoming events sidebar
Esc Close Close the modal or sidebar if open

CSS
